2. Natural Honey Exportation

Extraction

Our honey is taken straight from the beehives, collected by our apiarist from Uganda's virgin forest floras and 100% Natural.

Our honey has not been genetically modified or processed in any way which helps to retain its active properties and natural nutrition to the highest level possible. The extraction process is typically done inside a specialized room with all of the necessary tools nearby and is washable

Product Exportation

Over time, our honey products have been exported to many different countries in the world because of the quality standards we attach to our honey products.
